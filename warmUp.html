Warm Up Guide

Warm-up exercises are light physical activities performed before more intense exercise or sports. They help prepare the body by gradually increasing heart rate, improving flexibility, and enhancing circulation to muscles. Typical warm-up exercises include gentle movements like jogging, stretching, and dynamic motions that target the muscles to be used during the main activity. Proper warm-up reduces the risk of injury and improves overall performance
